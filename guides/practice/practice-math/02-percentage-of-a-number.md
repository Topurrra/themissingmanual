# Percentage of a number

A percentage is just a fraction of 100. To find "15% of 240," convert 15%
into a decimal (`15 / 100 = 0.15`) and multiply: `240 * 0.15`. That's the
whole trick - every "what's X% of Y" question is one multiplication once the
percent is written as a decimal.

The starter expression shows the shape (10% of 50) - it isn't this lesson's
answer.

**Your task:** what is 15% of 240?

**You'll practice:**

- Converting a percentage to a decimal before multiplying
- Writing a two-step calculation as a single expression
