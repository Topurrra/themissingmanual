# Physics Practice

Five short, hands-on physics lessons - same expression-editor format as Math
Practice, just with formulas pulled straight out of
[/guides/energy-forces-and-motion](/guides/energy-forces-and-motion) and
[/guides/what-physics-actually-is](/guides/what-physics-actually-is): Newton's
second law, speed, free-fall distance, momentum, and kinetic energy. Each
lesson gives you a real-world scenario with numbers already substituted in -
you write the expression, run it, and get checked instantly.

Your result is checked by rounding it to 4 decimal places and comparing it to
the expected answer - you don't need to match decimal formatting exactly,
just get the math right.

Start with lesson 1. You can leave and come back any time - your code is saved locally.
